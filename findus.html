STAMFORD

Access By Road: Stamford is just off the A1 running north-south, about halfway between Leeds and London, and accessible via the A47/A43 going east-west.

Access By Train: Stamford has its own railway station served by Cross Country Trains with connections from Peterborough, Leicester, Cambridge and Birmingham.
