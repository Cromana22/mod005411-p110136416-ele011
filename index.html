  Welcome To "Hello Stamford!"  

Welcome to "Hello Stamford", your one stop shop for all things related to Stamford, Lincolnshire, UK!

A small town with a large history and an even larger diary of events, there is something for everyone here. Visit "Events" page to see the upcoming festivals and time-limited attractions. Our "History To See" pages highlights some of the key historical landmarks for those with more cultural interests, while "Places To Stay" and "Food and Drink" will advise on the best places to extend your trip with a meal and overnight accomodation. Finally, "Find Us" gives you directions for all major transport types and can even let you know the weather to expect when you get here. So pull up a chair and plan your next trip out - we can't wait to meet you!
